About

Undergraduate students rarely have in hands books because of its cost. Finding trustworthy references becomes a very complicated situation, especially on the internet. Plus, The students have to know the contents of each syllabus demanded by the professors. And that's the problem we attack with our product. The project's goal is to focus on giving support to undergraduate students that have learning difficulties by a web platform. The material elaborated are class specific. How so? The materials will be based in the class syllabus and the professor's demands of that class. So we will have a colaborative network for elaborating and sharing materials between users for their respective classes.
